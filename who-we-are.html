WHO WE ARE

 

Gemini Exports, established in 1996, is an ISO certified pharmaceutical trading company based in Mumbai, India.

We have been in the industry for nearly 30 years, committed to provide quality products and delivering them consistently, in a timely manner, to our clients all over the world.

Our portfolio comprises of 300+ products including active pharma ingredients (API’s), pellets, direct compression, excipients, and probiotic blends.

Although our expertise lies in cardiovascular, gastrointestinal, endocrinology, ophthalmic and psychiatry needs, we also offer a wide range of other related products to clients in countries spanning across South/Central America, Middle East and South East Asia, with a presence in nearly 250 countries.

 We partner with ISO Certified, quality manufacturers to ensure we send the best products to our customers with the primary goal of promoting holistic well-being and healthier lives.
